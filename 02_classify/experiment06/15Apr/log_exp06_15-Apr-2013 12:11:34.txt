help cumsum
 <strong>cumsum</strong> Cumulative sum of elements.
    For vectors, <strong>cumsum</strong>(X) is a vector containing the cumulative sum of
    the elements of X. For matrices, <strong>cumsum</strong>(X) is a matrix the same size
    as X containing the cumulative sums over each column.  For N-D
    arrays, <strong>cumsum</strong>(X) operates along the first non-singleton dimension.
 
    <strong>cumsum</strong>(X,DIM) works along the dimension DIM.
 
    Example: If X = [0 1 2
                     3 4 5]
 
    then cumsum(X,1) is [0 1 2  and cumsum(X,2) is [0 1  3
                         3 5 7]                     3 7 12]
